What Should I Buy, and How Do I Justify It?

A Cheat Sheet for New Library Managers.

Collection development can be overwhelming for a new library manager. There are so many books and media released every month, but both shelf space and budget limit what you can actually purchase. How do you make sure that you are serving patrons from all backgrounds, while also keeping up with the most in-demand releases?
This "cheat sheet" is designed to help with aquisitions for adult collections. The same principles would also apply to juvenile and young adult collections, though some of the specific tools and publications will differ. This is by no means comprehensive, and is definitely not a substitute for your own good judgement... but it may help give you a place to start.
